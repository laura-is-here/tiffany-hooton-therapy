Services

I offer therapy for individuals, couples, and families, including children and adolescents.

We may work well together if you are:

  • A child, adolescent, or adult experiencing family conflict due to issues of past trauma, gender, or sexual orientation
  • A transgender person, partner of a transgender person, or couple where one or more people are under going a gender transition
  • An activist dealing with burnout, sexual harassment, or other repression
